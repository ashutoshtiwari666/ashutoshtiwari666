About me

I am currently working as a postdoctoral research associate in the Neuromechanics Research Laboratory at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi. Before this, I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi with a Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering. I have a Master's in Biomedical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay. I am particularly interested in gait rehabilitation and investigating the neuromechanics of human locomotion particularly within lower limb prosthesis.
